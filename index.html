Dragon Story

Meet characters of this story

-Chapter 1-






Once upon a time
there was a old king
who lived in a
golden castle
with his
beautiful daughter.


One night
an ugly ogre
captured
the beautiful
princess
and locked her up
in his tall,
dark tower
‘Help me!’ ‘Roar!’





-Chapter 2-

The old king
was very sad.
He promised to give
a bag of gold
to the person that
rescued the princess.
‘Please save our princess!’
‘We’ll save
the princess!’
All the knights
in the land
wanted to rescue
the princess.
They rode to the tower
as fast as they could.
‘Help me!’ ‘Roar!’
The ugly ogre roared
with anger
when he saw theknights.
His roar wasso scary
that they rode away
as fast as they could.





-Chapter 3-




One day
a friendly dragon
was flying over
the ogre’s tower
when he heard
the princess
cry for help.
‘Help me!’
The dragon flew
down to the tower,
took a big fiery breath
and blew
the ogre far away
over the mountains
and into the ocean.
‘Come with me, princess.
Don’t be scared!’
‘Thank you
for saving me.’
‘My pleasure, princess.’



-Chapter 4-








The dragon rescued
the princess
from the tower
and gently put her
on his strong back.
They flew high
in the sky.
They flew over
the tower
and the castle,
over the mountains
and caves,
and out towards
the deep blue ocean.
‘Whee! I can fly!’






-Chapter 5-







The dragon and the princess
flew to the castle.

The king
was so happy to see the princess
he gave the dragon the bag of gold.


They all lived happily ever after.
‘Thank you for saving our princess!’
‘My pleasure!’




The End
